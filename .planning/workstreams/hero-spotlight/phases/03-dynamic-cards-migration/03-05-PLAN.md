<objective>
Build the 5 frontend card components that consume the 5 new resolvers from Plan 03, extend the `SpotlightCard` TypeScript union to 9 variants, extend `HeroSpotlightBlock.vue`'s v-if/v-else-if dispatch chain to all 9 types, and add the 5 new i18n sub-namespaces to en.json + ru.json. Purpose: Without this plan, the backend's new cards are invisible to users. The frontend's existing carousel runs but the 5 new card types fall into the `v-else` branch and render nothing (or worse, crash). This plan closes that gap so the carousel renders all 9 types correctly.

- 5 new `.vue` files + 5 co-located `.spec.ts` files
- Extended `types/spotlight.ts` (discriminated union)
- Extended `HeroSpotlightBlock.vue` (dispatch chain to 9 types)
- Extended `en.json` + `ru.json` (5 new sub-namespaces)
- Updated `HeroSpotlightBlock.spec.ts` if it grep-asserts the card count
</objective>
