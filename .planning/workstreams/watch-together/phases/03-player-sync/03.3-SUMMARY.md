### Task 2 — `frontend/web/src/components/player/RawPlayer.vue`

## Why the bridge call moved off the original anchor line

The plan said "replace the `void props.room` line", but `videoRef` is declared ~16 lines later than that anchor in both files (the anchor sits immediately after `defineProps`, while `videoRef` is in the state-declaration block). `<script setup>` `const` declarations don't hoist, so calling `usePlayerSyncBridge(videoRef, ...)` at the anchor location would be a ReferenceError at runtime.

Resolution: removed the anchor lines entirely (acceptance criterion: `grep -c "void props.room" == 0` ✔), and placed the bridge call exactly where it can see a declared `videoRef`. The acceptance criterion `grep -c "usePlayerSyncBridge(videoRef, props.room) == 1"` is satisfied verbatim.

This is a minor execution adjustment (Rule 3 — fixed a blocker), not a design change.
