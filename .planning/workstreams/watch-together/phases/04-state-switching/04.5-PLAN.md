<objective>
Route per-player user-driven switcher actions through the room handle when the player is mounted inside a Watch Together room. The composable's emit methods + the validated backend handlers (04.3) + the view's onStateChanged subscription (04.4) form the round-trip; this plan inserts the final piece: the single conditional at the top of each player's `selectEpisode` / `selectTranslation` user-click handler.

Per CONTEXT §Frontend §Per-player switcher routing: "if (props.room) room.emitChange...; else localState = ...;" — single conditional, no other changes to player logic.

Purpose: when a user clicks "next episode" in a Watch Together room, the click MUST flow through the backend so it can validate + broadcast. Mutating local state directly would diverge the room — exactly what Phase 4 forbids.

Output: 5 player files edited with a 4-6 line guard at the top of each user-click selector. The "programmatic" (`_selectEpisode` / `_selectTranslation`) variants are NOT touched — those are called from auto-pick paths and from the broadcast handler (which is the correct local-state mutation point).
</objective>
